This page aims to educate and inform people about the various characteristics of the Cordillera Region. It explores the various traditions, beliefs, cuisines, superstitions, languages, and many others. This website was created in goals of clearing stereotypes of the people of the Cordillera Region. Stereotypes such as Igorot people have tails, people from Kalinga are aggressive, Igorot means people from Baguio , and too many to mention. And at the same time, be of help to answer questions that people have about the Cordilleras and its culture.
